How it works

Three quick steps and you are playing.

01

One player holds the phone

A player from the other team reads the prompt and taps answers as they are guessed.

02

Guess out loud

The guessing team calls out answers while the player holding the phone reveals matching ones on screen.

03

Swap and repeat

When time is up, review the round, pass the phone across, and start the next turn.
