Privacy

Tap Ten privacy summary

Core gameplay stays local

Tap Ten is designed as a local, single-device party game. Core gameplay, bundled question packs, and saved settings do not require an account.

Question reports are optional

If you choose to use Flag question, the app may include question metadata, your selected reason, your optional note, the app version, and a timestamp when submitting that report for content review.

If delivery is temporarily unavailable, the report may be saved locally on the device and retried later.

No account required

Tap Ten does not require account creation to play. The app is not built around social feeds, advertising, or public profiles.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact tapten-reports@orfeasa.com.
